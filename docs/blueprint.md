# **App Name**: Benace Tech Hub

## Core Features:

- Laptop Catalog Display: Showcase a comprehensive catalog of laptops with detailed specifications, images, and pricing.
- Product Search & Filtering: Allow users to easily search for specific laptops and filter results by brand, price, specifications, etc.
- Shopping Cart & Checkout: Enable users to add selected laptops to a cart and proceed through a secure checkout process.
- Web Design Services Portfolio: Present information and examples of available web design services, including project showcases.
- Service Inquiry Form: Provide a contact form for users to inquire about web design services, detailing their requirements.
- AI Product Recommendation Tool: Utilize AI to recommend relevant laptops to users based on their viewing history and specified preferences.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated indigo (#4A3D6B) for a modern and trustworthy feel, providing good contrast against a light background.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated violet (#F6F5F9) to maintain a clean, professional aesthetic and enhance readability.
- Accent color: A vibrant sky blue (#3B89ED) to draw attention to interactive elements, calls-to-action, and key highlights.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, suitable for both technical specifications and service descriptions.
- Use a set of clean, line-based icons that visually represent technology (laptops, gears) and creative services (design tools, ideas), ensuring a cohesive visual language.
- Implement a modular, responsive grid layout for displaying products, with distinct, well-organized sections for service offerings to ensure clarity and easy navigation on all devices.
- Subtle, smooth transition effects on product card hovers, form submissions, and section scrolling to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
